# digital-arrest-project-
cybercrime project on digital arrest(IIT Roorkee)
Overview:
This project is based on the concept of digital arrest, an emerging cybercrime threat where scammers impersonate law enforcement officials to extort money from victims through digital platforms.

Objective:
The main objective of this project is to:

- Understand how digital arrest scams work
- Analyze their psychological and financial impact
- Study preventive measures and awareness strategies

Key Topics Covered

- Digital arrest and cyber extortion techniques
- Role of video communication in cybercrime
- Socio-economic impact of cyber fraud
- Legal and policy frameworks
- Cybersecurity awareness and prevention

The complete project report is available in this repository in PDF format.

Learning Outcomes

- Gained knowledge of cybersecurity threats
- Understood real-world cybercrime patterns
- Improved research and analytical skills
- Learned the importance of digital awareness
